🏛️ CAAQMS Stations

  • Laboratory-grade precision sensors
  • Multiple pollutants (PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, CO, O₃)
  • Climate-controlled environment required
  • High maintenance costs (₹2-3L/year per station)
  • Complex calibration procedures
  • Large physical footprint
  • Installation time: 2-3 weeks per station
  • Coverage: ~5-10 km radius (estimated)

🚀 Our Hybrid Sensors

  • Reference (4 units): Semi-professional grade, 4km range
  • Monitoring (63 units): Mid-grade optical, 3km range
  • PM2.5 and PM10 detection (primary pollutants)
  • Weather-resistant outdoor deployment
  • Low maintenance (₹2-3k/year per sensor)
  • Automatic self-calibration via reference sensors
  • Compact, easy installation (2-3 hours)
  • Mathematically calculated overlap (not assumed)

💡 Key Innovation: Overlap Accounting
